YSS Rebrand

This rebrand makes YSS a more welcoming space for teens aged 12-18, emphasizing growth and recovery while removing the stigma around seeking help. Drawing inspiration from popular therapy sites, it shifts the focus to healing and possibility, making YSS feel like a supportive community. The vibrant color palette remains but is softened with muted tones, replacing harsh red with soft pink and desaturating the orange and blue. The typography is updated for modern appeal and improved legibility, aligning with youth trends while maintaining YSSâ€™s approachable, growth-focused identity.

Research

I chose to redesign Youth and Shelter Service (YSS) after delving into their mission statement and brand values. I conducted a full brand audit. I felt their brand identity did not reflect these values.
